About CHN Industries

Decades of excellence in leather jacket manufacturing and global distribution

Our Story

Founded in 2007 with just 5 skilled workers, CHN Industries has grown from a small workshop in Sialkot to become one of Pakistan's leading leather jacket manufacturers and exporters. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create high-quality leather apparel that combines style, durability, and comfort.

What started as a modest operation has blossomed into a thriving enterprise serving clients across multiple countries. Our first international partnership in 2011 marked a turning point, proving that our commitment to quality could compete on the global stage. Over the years, we've expanded our facilities, invested in cutting-edge technology, and built a team of over 300 dedicated professionals who share our passion for excellence.

Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art 50,000 sqft manufacturing facility equipped with the latest machinery and technology. Our production capacity has grown exponentially, allowing us to serve clients worldwide while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that set us apart from larger competitors.
